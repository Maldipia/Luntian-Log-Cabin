HOW TO USE:
-----------

LOGIN:
1. Open calendar.html
2. Enter password: luntian2025
3. Click Login

ADD BOOKING (Method 1 - Quick Add):
1. Click on empty cell in calendar
2. Form opens with room and date pre-filled
3. Enter guest name and other details
4. Click "Save Booking"

ADD BOOKING (Method 2 - Full Form):
1. Click "Add Booking" button
2. Fill in all details
3. Click "Save Booking"

EDIT BOOKING:
1. Click on any booking in calendar
2. Modify details in form
3. Click "Save Booking"

DELETE BOOKING:
1. Click on booking to edit
2. Click "Delete" button
3. Confirm deletion

EXPORT TO EXCEL:
1. Click "Export Excel" button
2. File downloads automatically
3. Open in Excel or Google Sheets

IMPORT FROM CSV/EXCEL:
1. Download bookings from Airbnb/Booking.com as CSV
2. Click "Import CSV" button
3. Select file
4. Bookings automatically added

CHANGE PASSWORD:
1. Open calendar.html in text editor
2. Find line 26: const ADMIN_PASSWORD = "luntian2025";
3. Change to your new password
4. Save file
